Oyakodon

Home Lasagna Oyakodon

Description

Oyakodon is a Japanese dish made out of eggs, chicken, and the classic
Japanese mixture of mirin, sugar, and soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Japanese Holy Trio
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Scallions or Negi

Steps

  1. Saute onions on a medium heat.
  2. Put your chicken and saute until slightly browned.
  3. Pour in the Japanese Holy Trio.
  4. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add in your egg mixture and chopped scallions.
    Simmer for one minute.
